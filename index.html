    Our Distillery

    Since 2020, Crafting Excellence in Every Bottle

    OQAAF EMPIRE LTD began with a vision to create premium alcoholic beverages that stand apart in quality, taste, and sophistication. Our flagship product, Sharke Bitters, is a profound natural alcoholic drink, crafted from authentic natural ingredients farmed by traditional farmers in West Africa.

    We source our ingredients from sustainable growers around the world, employing traditional distillation methods combined with modern innovation. Each batch is carefully monitored to ensure consistency and excellence.

    Today, our portfolio has expanded to include a range of premium beverages, but our commitment to craftsmanship, quality, and responsible enjoyment remains unchanged.

    Our Vision

    To be a valued provider for our customers, and a model of safety and excellence in production with the passion to be the best.

    Our Mission

    To provide the best and pure organic locally made alcoholic beverages of choice by exceeding the expectations of our customers and be the company of hope for the Ghanaian youth by providing them jobs.
